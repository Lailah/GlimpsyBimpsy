About Us

We are a group of people with books willing to share with anyone who's willing to change or share their books as well. We work locally, connecting people in the area. However, if you're not nearby but want to participate, please contact us through the Contact page or sending us an email.
In the present moment, we're accepting books of both fiction and non-fiction, technical texts and design/architecture magazines. If you'd like to see another subject or you have material about other topics, please contact us.
